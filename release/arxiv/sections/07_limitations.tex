% While \method{} has proved significantly effective over a wide range of challenging tasks, we identify a few limitations of the current version of \method{}. First, \method{} relies on frontier base models that can handle relatively complex coding-agent workflows, making it difficult to fully deploy on local devices. As such, an exciting future direction is to explore the training of customized small models for \method{}. Another limitation is the lack of bootstrapped heterogeneity in agents for multi-agent evolution, as we initialize all agents identically and give them access to identical information. We believe that future work could consider injecting unique personalities and private information into each agent to elicit greater diversity, thereby leading to a more efficient evolutionary process.
